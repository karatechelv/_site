📏 Dimensional Measurement

Precise measurement of part dimensions using various tools to ensure compliance with drawings and technical specifications.

🔹 What is Dimensional Measurement?

Dimensional measurement is the process of determining the physical dimensions of a part such as length, width, height, diameter, angles, and tolerances using precision tools. These measurements are performed to ensure the part conforms to the drawing and technical specifications.

🔹 Measurement Tools

  • Caliper – Measures length, inner and outer diameter with 0.01–0.05 mm accuracy
  • Micrometer – Precision measurement with 0.001 mm accuracy
  • Go/No-Go Gauges – Quick tolerance checking
  • CMM – High-accuracy 3D measurement
  • Profilometer – Surface roughness measurement

🔹 Related Standards

  • ISO 9001 – Measurement process control
  • ISO 10012 – Measurement management systems
  • ASME Y14.5 – Geometric dimensioning and tolerancing
