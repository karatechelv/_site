👁️ Visual Inspection

Visual inspection methods for identifying surface defects, cracks, corrosion, and other visible anomalies in materials and components.

🔹 What is Visual Inspection?

Visual inspection is the most basic and widely used non-destructive testing method. It involves the direct observation of a component or material to identify surface defects, cracks, corrosion, dimensional irregularities, and other visible anomalies.

🔹 Tools Used

  • Magnifying Glass – For detailed observation
  • Borescope – For internal inspection
  • Inspection Mirror – For hard-to-reach areas
  • Calipers and Scales – For dimensional checks
  • Lighting Equipment – For proper illumination

🔹 Common Defects Detected

  • Cracks and Fractures
  • Corrosion and Rust
  • Surface Scratches and Scuffs
  • Dimensional Irregularities
  • Discoloration and Staining
  • Poor Surface Finish
