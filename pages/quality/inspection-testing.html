🔍 Inspection & Testing

Inspection and testing methods for quality control including visual, dimensional, NDT, and functional testing.

🔹 What is Inspection & Testing?

Inspection and testing are essential components of quality control. They ensure that products and materials meet specified requirements before, during, and after production. These activities help identify defects, verify compliance, and ensure customer satisfaction.

🔹 Types of Inspection

  • Visual Inspection – Surface defects, cracks, corrosion
  • Dimensional Inspection – Measurements using gauges and CMM
  • Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) – Ultrasonic, MPI, Dye Penetrant
  • Destructive Testing – Mechanical, tensile, impact testing
  • Electrical Testing – Continuity, insulation resistance, hi-pot

🔹 Testing Methods

  • Factory Acceptance Test (FAT) – At manufacturer's facility
  • Site Acceptance Test (SAT) – At customer's facility
  • Commissioning – System startup and functional verification
  • Performance Testing – Under load conditions
