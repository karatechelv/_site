Bill of Materials (BOM)

Creating and managing Bill of Materials for electrical and control system projects.

What is a Bill of Materials?

A Bill of Materials (BOM) is a comprehensive list of all components, materials, and items required to manufacture, assemble, or construct a system. It includes part numbers, descriptions, quantities, and sourcing information.

BOM Structure

  • Item Number – Unique identifier for each component
  • Description – Detailed component description
  • Quantity – Number required for the project
  • Manufacturer/Supplier – Source of the component
  • Part Number – Manufacturer's part number
  • Unit Price – Cost per unit (for estimating)
  • Total Price – Quantity × Unit Price
  • Lead Time – Delivery time for procurement
