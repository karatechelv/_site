Design Learning Path

A structured learning path from foundation to advanced topics in engineering design.

Foundation Level

  • Introduction to Engineering Design
  • Basic Electrical & Control Systems
  • CAD Fundamentals (AutoCAD Basics)
  • Drawing Standards & Conventions

Intermediate Level

  • Electrical Schematics & One-Line Diagrams
  • Panel Layout & Enclosure Design
  • Cable Routing & Cable Schedules
  • BOM Creation & Management
  • AutoCAD Electrical / EPLAN Basics

Advanced Level

  • Advanced CAD (EPLAN Pro Panel, AutoCAD Plant 3D)
  • Instrumentation & P&ID Design
  • 3D Panel Design & Wire Harness
  • Project Documentation & Management
  • Integration with PLM Systems

Suggested Path Flow

Foundation → CAD Basics → Schematics → Panel Design → Cable Routing → BOM → Advanced CAD

Each step connects to previous, related, and next topics for fluid learning.
