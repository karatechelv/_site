Overview of Engineering Design

A comprehensive overview of engineering design, covering schematics, panel layouts, cable routing, and CAD tools.

What is Engineering Design?

Engineering design is the process of creating technical drawings, specifications, and documentation for electrical and control systems. It involves translating system requirements into detailed designs that can be manufactured, installed, and maintained.

Key Design Areas

  • Electrical Schematics – One-line, three-line, wiring diagrams
  • Panel Layouts – Enclosure design, component placement
  • Cable Routing – Trays, conduits, cable schedules
  • Bill of Materials – Component lists and procurement
  • Documentation – Manuals, test procedures, as-built drawings

Design Workflow

  • Project Requirements → Functional Specifications
  • Concept Design → Schematics & Drawings
  • Review & Approval → Procurement
  • FAT & Installation → As-built Documentation
