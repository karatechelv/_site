Engineering Design Software

Essential software tools for engineering design including CAD, schematics, panel design, and documentation.

CAD Software

  • AutoCAD – General-purpose 2D/3D drafting
  • AutoCAD Electrical – Electrical schematics and panel design
  • EPLAN Electric P8 – Advanced electrical engineering design
  • SolidWorks – 3D mechanical design
  • Inventor – 3D product design

Panel Design Software

  • EPLAN Pro Panel – 3D panel layout and wiring
  • AutoCAD Electrical – Panel layout and terminal plans
  • SEE Electrical – Electrical design and documentation
  • WSCAD – Panel design and schematics

Instrumentation & P&ID Software

  • AutoCAD Plant 3D – P&ID and plant design
  • Aveva / SmartPlant – P&ID and instrument design
  • COMOS – Lifecycle engineering
  • Smart Instrumentation – Instrument management

Documentation & Management Tools

  • Microsoft Excel – BOM, instrument index
  • Microsoft Access/SQL – Database management
  • SharePoint – Document management
  • Teamcenter – PLM (Product Lifecycle Management)

Example: Design Workflow with EPLAN

A typical engineering design workflow using EPLAN includes: Creating schematics → Generating panel layouts → Producing BOM → Exporting data for procurement → Creating terminal diagrams and cable schedules. All data is integrated in a single project database.
