IP Rating Guide

IP (Ingress Protection) ratings define the level of protection against solids and liquids. Essential for selecting equipment for different environments.

How to Use

Select the first digit (solid protection) and second digit (liquid protection). Click Lookup to see the IP rating and its description.

Example

IP67 = Protected against dust and temporary immersion in water. Suitable for outdoor use.
