Procurement

📖 Description

Procurement is the process of sourcing and purchasing all materials, components, and equipment needed for the project, including vendor selection and purchase orders.

📌 Example

Preparing a material take-off (MTO) for a control panel project, selecting vendors, and issuing purchase orders for PLC, relays, and wiring.

📚 Learning Path

  • Foundation: Understanding bill of materials (BOM)
  • Intermediate: Vendor selection and negotiation
  • Advanced: Supply chain management and logistics
