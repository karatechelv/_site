Overview of Engineering Workflow

A comprehensive overview of the complete engineering workflow from P&ID to Operation.

What is Engineering Workflow?

Engineering workflow is the sequence of activities and processes that transform a project from concept to operation. It covers all phases from design, procurement, assembly, testing, installation, commissioning, to final handover and operation.

Workflow Phases

  • P&ID – Process design and instrumentation
  • Engineering Design – Detailed design and schematics
  • Panel Design – Panel layout and wiring
  • Procurement – Material and component sourcing
  • Assembly – Panel assembly and wiring
  • FAT – Factory acceptance testing
  • Installation – On-site installation
  • Loop Check – Instrument loop verification
  • Commissioning – System startup and testing
  • SAT – Site acceptance testing
  • Documentation – As-built and operation manuals
  • Operation – System handover and operation

Workflow Integration

  • Project Management Integration – Schedule and resources
  • Quality Management Integration – Inspection and testing
  • Document Management Integration – Version control
  • Stakeholder Communication – Client and team coordination
