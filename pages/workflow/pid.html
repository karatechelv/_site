P&ID (Piping & Instrumentation Diagram)

📖 Description

P&ID is a detailed diagram showing the interconnection of process equipment and instrumentation. It is the foundation of any engineering project. P&IDs are used for design, construction, and operation of industrial plants.

📌 Example

P&ID for a chemical reactor showing temperature control loop (TIC-101), pressure indicator (PI-102), flow meter (FT-103), and control valve (FV-104). Includes all piping, fittings, and equipment connections.

📚 Learning Path

  • Foundation: Learn P&ID symbols and conventions (ISA 5.1)
  • Intermediate: Reading and interpreting P&IDs, understanding process flow
  • Advanced: Creating P&IDs with AutoCAD P&ID or EPLAN

💻 Software

  • AutoCAD P&ID
  • EPLAN
  • SmartPlant P&ID
  • Microsoft Visio (basic)
