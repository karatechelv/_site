Operation

📖 Description

Operation is the final phase where the system is used for its intended purpose. It includes normal operation, monitoring, and planned maintenance.

📌 Example

Operating a wastewater treatment plant: monitoring pumps, adjusting chemical dosing, and ensuring compliance with environmental standards.
