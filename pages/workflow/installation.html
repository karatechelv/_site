Installation

📖 Description

Installation is the process of mounting, wiring, and commissioning equipment at the customer site. It includes cable pulling, termination, and initial testing.

📌 Example

Installing a PLC control panel at a factory: mounting, routing cables, connecting field devices, and verifying power and communication.

📚 Learning Path

  • Foundation: Installation standards and safety
  • Intermediate: Cable routing and termination
  • Advanced: Coordination with civil and mechanical teams
