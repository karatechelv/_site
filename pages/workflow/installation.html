Installation Phase

On-site installation of panels, equipment, and cabling.

What is Installation?

Installation is the process of physically placing and connecting panels, equipment, and cabling at the customer's site. It involves mounting panels, pulling cables, making terminations, and preparing for system startup.

Installation Steps

  • Site Preparation – Location, foundations, access
  • Panel Mounting – Positioning and anchoring
  • Power Cable Installation – Routing and termination
  • Control Cable Installation – Instrument and I/O wiring
  • Grounding System – Earthing connections
  • Communication Cabling – Network connections
  • Final Inspection – Check before energizing

Installation Safety

  • LOTO Procedures – Isolation of energy sources
  • PPE – Hard hat, gloves, safety glasses
  • Working at Heights – Fall protection
  • Electrical Safety – Lockout/tagout
  • Permits – Work permit systems

Example: MCC Panel Installation

An MCC panel installation includes: placing the panel on prepared foundations, connecting incoming power cables, connecting outgoing motor cables, installing communication cables, and completing all grounding connections. A final inspection is performed before energizing.
