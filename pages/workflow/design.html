Engineering Design Phase

The design phase of the engineering workflow from concept to detailed engineering.

What is Engineering Design?

Engineering design is the process of developing detailed technical specifications, drawings, and documentation based on the P&ID and project requirements. It translates process requirements into a complete design package for procurement, assembly, and installation.

Design Deliverables

  • Electrical Schematics – One-line, three-line diagrams
  • Panel Layouts – Component placement and wiring
  • Loop Diagrams – Instrument wiring details
  • Cable Schedules – Cable routing and sizing
  • Bill of Materials – Component list and specifications
  • Technical Specifications – Equipment and materials

Design Process

  • Conceptual Design – Preliminary design and review
  • Detailed Engineering – Complete design package
  • Design Review – Client/team approval
  • Design Freeze – Final version for procurement
  • As-built Design – Final documentation after installation

Example: Design Package for MCC Panel

An MCC panel design package includes: single-line diagram, panel layout, wiring diagrams, cable schedule, bill of materials, and technical specifications. All documents are reviewed and approved before procurement begins.
