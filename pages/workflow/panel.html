Panel Design

📖 Description

Panel design involves selecting the right enclosure, components, and layout for control panels. This includes sizing, heat dissipation, and wiring considerations.

📌 Example

Designing a PLC control panel with 24V power supply, input/output modules, and Ethernet communication.

📚 Learning Path

  • Foundation: Panel components and their functions
  • Intermediate: Layout design and component sizing
  • Advanced: Complete panel design with EPLAN Pro Panel
