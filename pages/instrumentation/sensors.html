📡 Sensors & Transmitters

Industrial sensors and transmitters for measuring temperature, pressure, flow, level, and other process variables.

What are Sensors and Transmitters?

Sensors detect physical changes in the process (temperature, pressure, flow, level). Transmitters convert sensor signals into standardized outputs (4-20mA, HART, etc.) for control systems.

🔹 Temperature

  • Thermocouples (Type K, J, T)
  • RTD (PT100)
  • Thermistors (NTC, PTC)
  • Temperature Transmitters

🔹 Pressure

  • Piezoelectric Sensors
  • Strain Gauge Transducers
  • Capacitive Pressure Sensors
  • Differential Pressure Transmitters

🔹 Flow & Level

  • Magnetic Flow Meters (Magflow)
  • Coriolis Mass Flow Meters
  • Ultrasonic Flow Meters
  • Radar Level Sensors
  • Ultrasonic Level Sensors

🔹 Example System

A PT100 RTD sensor measures temperature in a reactor. The signal goes to a temperature transmitter, which outputs 4-20mA to the PLC/DCS for control and monitoring.
