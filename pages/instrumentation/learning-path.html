Instrumentation Learning Path

A structured learning path from foundation to advanced topics in instrumentation engineering.

Foundation Level

  • Introduction to Instrumentation & Control
  • Basic Physics – Pressure, Temperature, Flow, Level
  • Electrical Fundamentals for Instrumentation
  • Process Control Basics – Open/Closed Loop

Intermediate Level

  • Sensors & Transmitters (Pressure, Temp, Flow, Level)
  • 4-20mA and HART Communication
  • Control Valves and Actuators
  • Loop Diagrams and Hook-up Drawings
  • Calibration Procedures

Advanced Level

  • Smart Transmitters and Digital Communication (Fieldbus)
  • Process Analyzers (pH, Conductivity, Gas)
  • Safety Instrumented Systems (SIS)
  • Functional Safety (IEC 61508/61511)
  • Advanced Process Control (APC)

Suggested Path Flow

Foundation → Sensors & Transmitters → Communication Protocols → Control Valves → Loop Diagrams → Calibration → Analyzers → Safety & Advanced

Each step connects to previous, related, and next topics for fluid learning.
