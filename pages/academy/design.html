Design Engineering

CAD, schematics, panel design, cable routing, and electrical layout design. Covers everything from concept to detailed engineering drawings.

Overview

Design engineering is the process of creating detailed technical drawings and specifications for electrical and automation systems. It transforms concepts into buildable designs.

Key Topics

  • Electrical schematics and single-line diagrams
  • Panel layout and wiring diagrams
  • Cable routing and tray sizing
  • Terminal charts and cable schedules
  • Bill of materials (BOM) and documentation
