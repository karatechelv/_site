Quality Control

Inspection, testing, ISO standards, and continuous improvement for engineering excellence.
Inspection & Testing
  • Visual inspection of components
  • Dimensional measurement
  • Electrical testing
  • Functional testing
  • Pressure testing
  • Leak testing
All testing procedures follow IEC and ISO standards.
Standards & Certifications
  • ISO 9001:2015 - Quality Management
  • ISO 14001 - Environmental Management
  • ISO 45001 - Occupational Health & Safety
  • IEC 61508 - Functional Safety
  • ATEX / IECEx - Hazardous Areas
Certification process includes documentation, audit, and continuous compliance.
QC Tools & Techniques
  • Pareto Chart
  • Fishbone (Ishikawa) Diagram
  • Check Sheets
  • Histograms
  • Control Charts (SPC)
  • Scatter Diagrams
  • Flowcharts
These 7 basic tools are the foundation of quality improvement.
Improvement Methodologies
  • Six Sigma (DMAIC)
  • Lean Manufacturing
  • Kaizen (Continuous Improvement)
  • 5S - Workplace Organization
  • PDCA Cycle
  • TPM - Total Productive Maintenance
These methodologies help reduce waste, defects, and increase efficiency.
Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)
  • Ultrasonic Testing (UT)
  • Radiographic Testing (RT - X-ray)
  • Dye Penetrant Testing (PT)
  • Magnetic Particle Testing (MT)
  • Eddy Current Testing (ET)
  • Visual Testing (VT)
NDT methods are used to detect defects without damaging the component.
Documentation & Audits
  • Quality Manuals
  • Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)
  • Work Instructions
  • Inspection & Test Records
  • Internal Audits
  • External Audits (Third Party)
  • Corrective & Preventive Actions (CAPA)
Proper documentation ensures traceability and compliance with standards.
Statistical Quality Control (SQC)
  • Acceptance Sampling
  • Process Capability Analysis (Cp, Cpk)
  • Control Charts (X-bar, R, P, NP)
  • ANOVA - Analysis of Variance
  • Regression Analysis
SQC uses statistical methods to monitor and control quality.
Supplier Quality Management
  • Supplier Audits
  • Supplier Evaluation & Selection

  • Supplier Performance Monitoring
  • Supplier Development
Supplier quality management ensures raw materials and components meet standards.
