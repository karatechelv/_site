Quality Control

Inspection, testing, ISO standards, and continuous improvement for engineering excellence.
Inspection & Testing
  • Visual inspection of components and assemblies
  • Dimensional measurement with calipers, micrometers, CMM
  • Electrical testing: continuity, insulation resistance, hi-pot
  • Functional testing under real operating conditions
SPC - Statistical Process Control
  • Control charts (X-bar, R, p, np, c, u)
  • Process capability analysis (Cp, Cpk, Pp, Ppk)
  • Identifying common cause vs special cause variation
  • Pareto analysis for prioritizing improvements
ISO 9001:2015
  • Quality Management System (QMS) requirements
  • Documentation: quality policy, manual, procedures
  • Internal audit and management review process
  • Certification process and surveillance audits
7 QC Tools
  • Pareto Chart – 80/20 rule for prioritization
  • Fishbone (Ishikawa) Diagram – Root cause analysis
  • Check Sheets – Data collection and tallying
  • Histograms – Frequency distribution visualization
  • Control Charts – Monitoring process variation
  • Scatter Diagrams – Relationship between two variables
  • Flowcharts – Process mapping and standardization
Six Sigma
  • DMAIC methodology (Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, Control)
  • Defect reduction and process optimization
  • Achieving 3.4 defects per million opportunities (DPMO)
  • Six Sigma Belts: Yellow, Green, Black, Master Black Belt
Quality Audits
  • Internal audits – Self-assessment and improvement
  • External audits – Customer and certification body audits
  • Audit planning, checklists, and evidence collection
  • Non-conformance reporting and corrective action
NDT - Non-Destructive Testing
  • Ultrasonic testing – Detecting internal flaws using sound waves
  • Radiography (X-ray) – Internal inspection of welds and castings
  • Dye penetrant – Surface crack detection
  • Magnetic particle – Detecting surface and near-surface defects
  • Eddy current – Electrical conductivity and coating thickness
Quality Documentation
  • Quality manuals – Overview of the QMS
  • Procedures – Detailed process descriptions
  • Work instructions – Step-by-step task guidance
  • Inspection records and calibration certificates
  • Non-conformance reports and corrective action records
