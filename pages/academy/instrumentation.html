Instrumentation Engineering

Sensors, transmitters, control valves, analyzers, and field instrumentation. Essential for measuring and controlling process variables in industrial plants.

Overview

Instrumentation engineering focuses on the design, installation, and maintenance of instruments used to measure and control pressure, temperature, flow, level, and other process variables.
