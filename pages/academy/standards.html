Standards Engineering

IEC, NEC, ISO, API, IEEE standards application in engineering projects. Essential for ensuring safety, reliability, and compliance in industrial projects.

Overview

Standards engineering involves understanding and applying international and national standards to ensure that engineering designs, products, and systems meet safety, quality, and performance requirements.

Key Standards

  • IEC 60364 (Electrical installations)
  • IEC 61439 (Switchgear and MCC)
  • ISO 9001 (Quality management)
  • API RP 14C (Safety systems)
  • IEEE 1584 (Arc flash safety)
