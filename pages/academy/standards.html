Engineering Standards

IEC, ISO, NEC, IEEE standards for electrical, instrumentation, and automation engineering.
IEC Standards
  • IEC 60364 - Electrical Installations
  • IEC 61508 - Functional Safety
  • IEC 61511 - Process Safety
  • IEC 61850 - Communication for Substations
  • IEC 60529 - IP Codes
  • IEC 61000 - EMC Standards
IEC standards are the most widely used international electrical standards.
ISO Standards
  • ISO 9001 - Quality Management
  • ISO 14001 - Environmental Management
  • ISO 45001 - Occupational Health & Safety
  • ISO 50001 - Energy Management
  • ISO 31000 - Risk Management
  • ISO 22000 - Food Safety
ISO standards provide frameworks for quality and management systems.
NEC / NFPA Standards
  • NFPA 70 - National Electrical Code (NEC)
  • NFPA 72 - Fire Alarm & Signaling
  • NFPA 70E - Electrical Safety in Workplace
  • NFPA 79 - Industrial Machinery
  • NFPA 110 - Emergency & Standby Power
  • NFPA 101 - Life Safety Code
NEC is the primary electrical standard in the United States.
IEEE Standards
  • IEEE 1584 - Arc Flash Calculation
  • IEEE 519 - Harmonics Control
  • IEEE 802 - LAN/MAN Standards
  • IEEE 1459 - Power Quality
  • IEEE C37 - Switchgear Standards
  • IEEE 1057 - Digitizing Waveform
IEEE standards are widely used in electrical and electronics engineering.
ISA Standards
  • ISA-5.1 - Instrument Symbols
  • ISA-5.2 - Binary Logic Diagrams
  • ISA-5.4 - Instrument Loop Diagrams
  • ISA-88 - Batch Control
  • ISA-95 - Enterprise-Control Integration
  • ISA-99 - Cybersecurity
ISA standards are essential for instrumentation and automation.
ATEX / IECEx Standards
  • ATEX 114 - Equipment for Explosive Atmospheres
  • ATEX 153 - Workplace Safety
  • IECEx Certification Scheme
  • Hazardous Area Classification
  • Ex Equipment Marking
  • Explosion Protection Techniques (Ex d, Ex e, Ex i)
ATEX and IECEx ensure safety in explosive atmospheres.
VDE Standards
  • VDE 0100 - Electrical Installations
  • VDE 0110 - Insulation Coordination
  • VDE 0141 - Earthing Systems
  • VDE 0550 - Transformers
  • VDE 0635 - Protection Relays
  • VDE 0710 - Lighting Installations
VDE standards are widely used in Germany and Europe.
BS Standards
  • BS 7671 - UK Wiring Regulations
  • BS EN 61439 - Switchgear Assemblies
  • BS 5839 - Fire Detection Systems
  • BS 5266 - Emergency Lighting
  • BS 6231 - Cable Standards
  • BS 6460 - Test Methods
BS standards are used in the United Kingdom and many other countries.
