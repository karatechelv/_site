Overview of Electrical Engineering

Electrical engineering is the core of modern infrastructure. It covers everything from power generation to end-user consumption. This field encompasses the design, analysis, and maintenance of electrical systems that power industries, cities, and technologies.

  • Power Generation: Converting energy sources into electrical power.
  • Transmission & Distribution: Delivering power from plants to consumers.
  • Protection & Control: Ensuring system safety and reliability.
  • Industrial Installations: Electrical systems for factories and commercial buildings.
📌 Key Standards: IEC 60364, NEC, IEEE
