Overview of Engineering Management

A comprehensive overview of engineering management, covering project management, risk, workflow, and leadership.

What is Engineering Management?

Engineering management is the discipline that combines engineering knowledge with business and management skills. It involves planning, organizing, directing, and controlling engineering projects and teams to achieve organizational goals efficiently and effectively.

Key Management Areas

  • Project Management – Planning, scheduling, execution
  • Risk Management – Identification, assessment, mitigation
  • Workflow Management – Process optimization
  • Resource Management – People, materials, budget
  • Leadership – Team building, communication, motivation

Management Frameworks

  • PMBOK – Project Management Body of Knowledge
  • PRINCE2 – Project Management Methodology
  • ISO 21500 – Guidance on Project Management
  • Agile – Adaptive project management
  • Lean – Waste reduction and efficiency
