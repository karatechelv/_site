Management Learning Path

A structured learning path from foundation to advanced topics in engineering management.

Foundation Level

  • Introduction to Engineering Management
  • Basic Management Concepts – Planning, Organizing, Leading, Controlling
  • Project Management Fundamentals
  • Communication & Teamwork

Intermediate Level

  • Project Scheduling & Planning (Gantt, CPM, PERT)
  • Risk Management & Mitigation
  • Resource Planning & Allocation
  • Workflow Design & Optimization
  • Quality Management (ISO 9001)

Advanced Level

  • Strategic Engineering Management
  • Leadership & Organizational Behavior
  • Financial Management for Engineers
  • Change Management
  • Portfolio & Program Management

Suggested Path Flow

Foundation → Project Fundamentals → Scheduling & Risk → Resource & Workflow → Quality & Leadership → Strategic Management

Each step connects to previous, related, and next topics for fluid learning.
