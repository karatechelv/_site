Project Management

Planning, scheduling, execution, and monitoring of engineering projects to ensure successful delivery.

What is Project Management?

Project management is the application of knowledge, skills, tools, and techniques to project activities to meet project requirements. It involves initiating, planning, executing, controlling, and closing projects to achieve specific goals within constraints of time, budget, and quality.

Project Management Processes

  • Initiation – Define project goals and scope
  • Planning – Create detailed plan and schedule
  • Execution – Implement the plan
  • Monitoring & Control – Track progress and adjust
  • Closure – Finalize and handover

Project Scheduling Tools

  • Gantt Charts – Visual timeline of activities
  • Critical Path Method (CPM) – Identify critical tasks
  • PERT Charts – Activity network diagrams
  • Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) – Task hierarchy
