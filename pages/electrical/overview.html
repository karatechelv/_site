📘 Overview of Electrical Engineering

A comprehensive overview of electrical engineering, covering power systems, protection, design, and standards.

🔹 What is Electrical Engineering?

Electrical engineering is a field of engineering that deals with the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. It covers generation, transmission, distribution, and utilization of electrical power.

🔹 Main Branches

  • Power Systems – Generation, transmission, distribution
  • Control Systems – Automation and process control
  • Electronics – Analog and digital circuits
  • Telecommunications – Signal processing and communication

🔹 Key Standards

  • IEC 60364 – Electrical Installations
  • IEC 61439 – Switchgear
  • IEEE 80 – Earthing
  • NEC (NFPA 70)
