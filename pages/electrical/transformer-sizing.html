Calculate transformer sizing, voltage regulation, and full-load currents for primary and secondary windings.

What is Transformer Sizing?

Transformer sizing is the process of selecting a transformer with the appropriate kVA rating to handle the expected load. It involves calculating the load requirements, considering factors such as future expansion, efficiency, and voltage regulation.

Key Formula

I = S / (√3 × V)
  • S = Transformer Rating (kVA)
  • V = Voltage (V)
  • I = Current (A)

Key Factors in Transformer Selection

  • Load Requirements
  • Efficiency and Losses
  • Voltage Regulation
  • Cooling Method (ONAN, ONAF, etc.)
  • Future Expansion
