🔌 Transformer Sizing

How to properly size transformers for electrical systems based on load and application.

Transformer Basics

Transformers are essential components in power systems, used to step up or step down voltage levels. Proper sizing ensures efficient and reliable operation.

Sizing Criteria

Transformer sizing depends on load type, power factor, voltage levels, and future expansion. Key parameters include kVA rating, impedance, and cooling method.

Common Standards

IEC 60076 and IEEE C57.12 are common standards for transformer design, testing, and application.
