🌍 Earthing / Grounding

Complete guide to earthing systems, resistance calculation, and reference tables.

What is Earthing?

Earthing (grounding) is the process of connecting electrical equipment to the earth to ensure safety, protect against electric shock, and provide a reference point for electrical systems.
