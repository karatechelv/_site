🌍 Earthing Resistance

Calculate earth resistance for different electrode types and soil resistivity.

What is Earthing Resistance?

Earthing resistance is the resistance between an earthing electrode and the earth. A low earthing resistance is essential for safety, equipment protection, and lightning protection.

Key Formula

R = (ρ / 2πL) × ln(4L/d)
  • ρ = Soil Resistivity (Ω·m)
  • L = Electrode Length (m)
  • d = Electrode Diameter (m)
  • R = Earth Resistance (Ω)

Types of Earthing Electrodes

  • Rod Electrodes
  • Plate Electrodes
  • Mesh Electrodes
  • Chemical Electrodes
