📈 Power System Analysis

Load flow, short-circuit, stability, and protection studies.

🔹 Load Flow Analysis

Load flow analysis determines the voltage, current, and power flow in a power system under steady-state conditions. It is essential for planning and operation.

🔹 Short-Circuit Analysis

Short-circuit studies are performed to determine the fault currents and select appropriate protective devices. They ensure system safety and equipment protection.

🔹 Stability Analysis

Power system stability refers to the ability of the system to maintain synchronism after a disturbance. Transient and voltage stability are key aspects.

🔹 Key Studies

  • Load flow (power flow)
  • Short-circuit (fault) analysis
  • Transient stability
  • Voltage stability
  • Protection coordination
