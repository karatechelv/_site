📏 Cable Sizing

Selecting the correct cable size based on current, voltage drop, and installation conditions.

Why Cable Sizing Matters

Proper cable sizing ensures safety, minimizes voltage drop, prevents overheating, and reduces energy losses in electrical systems.

Key Factors

  • Current carrying capacity (ampacity)
  • Voltage drop limits
  • Installation method and ambient temperature
  • Short-circuit withstand rating

Standards

  • IEC 60364-5-52 – Cable sizing
  • NEC Article 310
  • BS 7671
