What is Cable Sizing?

Cable sizing is the process of selecting the correct conductor cross-sectional area to safely carry the expected current without overheating or excessive voltage drop.

Proper cable sizing ensures safety, reduces energy losses, and prevents equipment damage. It involves considering factors such as ambient temperature, installation method, and grouping of cables.
