🗺️ Learning Path

From foundation to advanced electrical engineering. A structured learning roadmap.

📘 Foundation Level

  • Basic electrical concepts (Voltage, Current, Resistance)
  • Ohm's Law and Kirchhoff's Laws
  • AC/DC fundamentals
  • Basic circuit analysis

📗 Intermediate Level

  • Power systems and load flow
  • Transformer and motor theory
  • Protection and relay coordination
  • Cable sizing and voltage drop

📕 Advanced Level

  • Power system analysis and fault studies
  • Earthing design and safety
  • SCADA and automation systems
  • Project management and standards

📌 Suggested Roadmap

1. Basics → 2. Circuits → 3. Power → 4. Protection → 5. Design → 6. Automation → 7. Advanced
