Learning Path in Electrical Engineering

A structured learning path from foundation to advanced levels in electrical engineering.

Foundation Level

  • Basic Electrical Theory
  • Circuit Analysis
  • Ohm's Law and Kirchhoff's Laws
  • AC and DC Systems
  • Introduction to Electrical Safety
📌 Recommended Books: Electrical Engineering 101, Basic Circuit Analysis

Intermediate Level

  • Power Systems Engineering
  • Protection Coordination
  • Transformers and Motors
  • Electrical Installation Design
  • Lighting and Earthing Systems
📌 Recommended Books: Power System Analysis, Electrical Installation Guide

Advanced Level

  • Power System Studies
  • ETAP and Advanced Software
  • High-Voltage Engineering
  • Smart Grid and Microgrids
  • Renewable Energy Integration
📌 Recommended Books: Smart Grid, Power System Protection

Practical Skills

  • CAD and EPLAN for Electrical Design
  • Site Supervision and Installation
  • Testing and Commissioning
  • Project Management
