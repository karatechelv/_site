⚡ Power Systems

Generation, transmission, distribution, and analysis of electrical power systems.

🔹 Introduction

Power systems are networks of electrical components used to supply, transfer, and use electric power. They include generation plants, transmission lines, substations, and distribution networks.

🔹 Key Components

  • Generators – Convert mechanical energy to electrical
  • Transformers – Step up/down voltage
  • Transmission lines – High voltage power transfer
  • Distribution networks – Low voltage supply to consumers

🔹 Analysis Methods

  • Load flow analysis
  • Short-circuit analysis
  • Stability studies
  • Protection coordination
