Standards Reference

Quick reference for key engineering standards: IEC, NEC, ISO, API, and IEEE.

IEC Standards

  • IEC 61439 – Switchgear and controlgear assemblies
  • IEC 60204 – Electrical equipment of machines
  • IEC 60364 – Electrical installations of buildings
  • IEC 61508 – Functional safety of E/E/PE systems
  • IEC 61511 – Safety instrumented systems
  • IEC 61131-3 – PLC programming languages
  • IEC 61784 – Industrial communication networks

NEC Standards

  • NFPA 70 – National Electrical Code
  • NFPA 70E – Electrical Safety in the Workplace
  • NFPA 79 – Electrical Standard for Industrial Machinery
  • NFPA 110 – Standard for Emergency and Standby Power

ISO Standards

  • ISO 9001 – Quality Management Systems
  • ISO 14001 – Environmental Management Systems
  • ISO 45001 – Occupational Health & Safety
  • ISO 50001 – Energy Management
  • ISO 17025 – Testing and Calibration Laboratories
  • ISO 21500 – Project Management Guidance

API & IEEE Standards

  • API RP 551 – Process Measurement Instrumentation
  • API RP 554 – Process Instrumentation and Control
  • API Q1 – Quality Management for Oil & Gas
  • IEEE 80 – Earthing and Grounding
  • IEEE 1584 – Arc Flash Hazard Calculations
  • IEEE 519 – Harmonic Control in Power Systems

Example: Applying Standards

When designing a control panel, refer to IEC 61439 for construction requirements, NEC NFPA 70 for installation, and IEEE 80 for grounding design. Compliance ensures safety, reliability, and regulatory acceptance.
